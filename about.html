Who am I?

To zeroth order, I am just another random nerd with a personal website. To first order, you can approximate me as a combination of music and physics, although I love learning just about anything. It is a wild ride. Physics, math, and Medtner have become especially attractive to me since coming to UCSB.

I'm a very outgoing and sociable person. Spontaneous both socially and intellectually. If you're curious about my professional self, you can check out my linkedin or github on the contact page, or my CV.
