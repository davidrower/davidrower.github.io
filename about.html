Who am I?

To zeroth order, I am just another random nerd with a personal website. To first order, you can approximate me as a combination of music and physics. I grew up playing the piano in Los Angeles, got my B.S. in Physics from UCSB, and am currently pursuing my PhD in experimental condensed matter physics as an NSF Fellow at MIT.
